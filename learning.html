Learning

Here's what I'm currently learning.

  • Healthcare

    I'm interested in healthcare and the ways we can improve human lives. There are huge opportunities to better our healthcare system in the US. Technology has a outsized impact here in (1) organizing and understanding big healthcare data (images, medical records, etc.) (2) providing a more patient-centric, compassionate healthcare experience (3) personalization of therapies and treatments (4) frequent passive signals into patient health via wearables (5) acceleration of clinical R&D (6) increasing access to care via telemedicine.

  • Artificial Intelligence

    I'm interested in artificial intelligence and it's applications. After all "Artificial intelligence is the new electricity." - Andrew Ng. I'm currently learning about deep learning and computer vision. I'm particularly interested in neural networks.

  • Brain Computer Interfaces

    I'm interested in brain computer interfaces. I'm fascinated by the potential applications in learning, medicine and gaming. I think brain computer interfaces have the potential to change how we perceive the world and how we think, learn, remember and transfer information to one another.     I'm curious about philosophy. I'm particularly interested in emergent properties of complex systems and organizations/organizational behavior.
